The Best Movie I've Seen

Although I enjoy watching movies both at the theatre and from home, I don't find myself watching them too often nowadays. However, one movie I have recently seen this past summer that I particularly enjoyed is Bullet Train. I tend to be more drawn towards serious-toned TV shows and films, but was convinced by my dad, who loves comedic films, to give this movie a chance. I was quite surprised at the fact that I enjoyed the movie as much as I did. Each person underwent significant character development that kept me invested in the plot-line. My favourite aspects of the movie was its pacing and cinematography. After watching this movie, I'm interested to know how much I would enjoy other movies made by the same director, David Leitch.
